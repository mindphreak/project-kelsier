# Prohibited Uses

This software and its derived insights may NOT be used for:

- Hiring or recruitment screening
- Performance ranking or scoring
- Compensation decisions
- Termination justification
- Academic grading of individuals
- Automated decision-making affecting livelihoods

Any system that attempts to reduce human complexity into
scores, rankings, or pass/fail judgments violates the spirit
of this project.

Forks violating these principles may not claim association,
endorsement, or lineage with this project.
